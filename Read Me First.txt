VIGIL — quick start
===================

1. Double-click "Install Vigil.command".
   (First time: right-click it, choose Open, then click Open.)
   Wait for the "Vigil is ready!" message.

2. Double-click "Vigil.app" to start.

WHAT IT DOES
------------
• Proximity lock: locks your screen when your phone leaves, unlocks nothing.
  - Pair your phone in System Settings > Bluetooth first.
  - In Vigil, pick your phone under "Device", then click "Arm".
  - Hover any control for a plain-English explanation.

• Motion alarm: sounds a siren if the Mac is moved while armed.
  - This part needs your password (the motion sensor is protected by macOS).
  - Start it from Terminal:
      sudo -E "<this folder>/.venv/bin/python3" "<this folder>/vigil.py"

NOTES
-----
• Vigil never unlocks your Mac — macOS doesn't allow apps to do that.
  Use Touch ID or Apple Watch to unlock quickly.
• Your settings save automatically and come back next time.
